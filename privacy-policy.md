  # Privacy Policy for ScoreBoard                                                                                                                                                                                                              
                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               
  **Effective Date:** March 4, 2026                                                                                                                                                                                                            
                                                            
  ## Introduction

  ScoreBoard ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our HAC grade tracking application.

  ## Information We Collect

  ### 1. Login Credentials (Stored Locally Only)
  - **HAC Username and Password**: Stored securely on your device using iOS/Android secure storage
  - **Never transmitted** to our servers
  - **Never shared** with third parties
  - **Only used** to authenticate with your school's HAC system
  - **Deleted** when you logout or uninstall the app

  ### 2. Anonymous Grade Sharing (Optional)

  When you **opt-in** to Grade Sharing, we collect:

  **What We Collect:**
  - Course code (e.g., "0708400")
  - Class name (e.g., "AP Spanish Language & Culture")
  - Your grade percentage (e.g., 84%)
  - Anonymous device fingerprint (cannot identify you personally)
  - School code (hashed identifier)

  **What We DON'T Collect:**
  - Your name
  - Student ID number
  - HAC username or password
  - Individual assignment scores
  - Any personally identifiable information (PII)

  **How It Works:**
  - Grades uploaded once per 24 hours when enabled
  - Used to calculate anonymous class averages
  - Stored in our secure database (Supabase)
  - Class averages shown only when 10+ students participate
  - You can toggle sharing on/off at any time
  - Your data is **immediately deleted** when you disable sharing

  ## How We Use Your Information

  ### Login Credentials
  - Used solely to authenticate with your school's HAC system
  - Processed entirely on your device
  - Never leave your device

  ### Grade Data (If Sharing Enabled)
  - Calculate anonymous class averages
  - Display comparative statistics (e.g., "5% above class average")
  - Improve understanding of class performance trends

  ## Data Storage and Security

  - **Login credentials**: Stored locally on your device using iOS Keychain / Android Keystore
  - **Grade data**: Stored in encrypted database with industry-standard security
  - **Anonymous IDs**: Cannot be traced back to individual students
  - **No tracking**: We don't track your app usage, location, or behavior

  ## Your Privacy Rights

  You have the right to:
  - **Opt-in or opt-out** of grade sharing at any time
  - **Delete your shared data** by disabling grade sharing
  - **Request information** about what data we store
  - **Uninstall the app** and all local data will be removed

  ## Data Retention

  - **Login credentials**: Stored until you logout or uninstall
  - **Grade data**: Retained while sharing is enabled; deleted immediately when disabled
  - **Class averages**: Recalculated daily; old submissions overwritten

  ## Third-Party Services

  We use:
  - **Supabase** (database hosting) - for anonymous grade data only
  - **Home Access Center (HAC)** - your school's official grade system (we don't control their privacy practices)

  We do NOT use:
  - Analytics or tracking services
  - Advertising networks
  - Social media integrations
  - Any service that collects personal information

  ## Children's Privacy

  ScoreBoard is designed for students. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone. Grade sharing is:
  - Completely optional (opt-in)
  - Anonymous (no personal data)
  - Protected (minimum 10 students for averages)

  ## Changes to This Policy

  We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:
  - Updating the "Effective Date" at the top
  - Displaying a notice in the app
  - Requiring re-acceptance for material changes

  ## Contact Us

  If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data:
  - **In-App**: Settings → Privacy Policy
  - **Email**: scoreboardhac@gmail.com

  ## Your Consent

  By using ScoreBoard, you consent to this Privacy Policy. For grade sharing specifically, you must explicitly opt-in via the Grade Sharing toggle in Settings.
